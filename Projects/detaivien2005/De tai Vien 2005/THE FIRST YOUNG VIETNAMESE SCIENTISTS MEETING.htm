FOREWORD

The first “Young Vietnamese Scientists Meeting 2005” (YVSM’05) is jointly organized by The College of Technology (COLTEC) of Vietnam National University (VNU), Hanoi and the Vietnam Education Foundation (VEF). Young Vietnamese scientists presently studying and working at any education or research institutions inside or outside of Vietnam are invited to participate. For all participants no conference fee is required.

The Meeting aims to provide a forum for young Vietnamese scientists to exchange and discuss their experiences and results from their studies, training, and research activities. This Meeting also aims to foster a worldwide and long-term channel of communication and cooperation among young Vietnamese scientists. The Meeting will be held from June 12-16, 2005 in Nha Trang, a well known seaside resort in Vietnam.

LANGUAGE

English is the official symposium language. Only in special cases may paper presentations be given in the Vietnamese language at the Meeting.

PRESENTATION DETAILS

The program will include the following:

  1. Thirty-minute plenary talks given by invited speakers followed by 10-minute discussion/Q&A;
  2. Fifteen-minute oral presentations followed by 5-minute discussion/ Q&A;
  3. Poster presentation.

An overhead projector and LCD projector will be available for all presenters. An area of about 0.90 m2 (0.80 by 1.20) will be provided for the presentation of posters.

A dialogue with prominent scientists at the Meeting will be organized.

abstract submission

 AND pAPER PUBLICATION PROCEDURES

Young scientists, lecturers, and post-docs (aged under 35) involved in one of the scientific areas highlighted above, as well as those holding either a Ph.D., MSc., or BSc., are invited to submit an abstract of their paper to the Organizing Committee for judging. All submissions should be made in English and will be judged for their originality, clarity, contribution to the relevant field of science, as well as relevance to the YVSM. Those selected to make presentations will be notified, after which they will be asked to submit their full paper to the Organizing Committee, which should not exceed six (6) A4 pages. The proceedings of the conference, including lectures delivered by guest speakers, as well as the accepted full papers, will be published by Vietnam National University Publishers after the conference closes. Please refer to the instructions and use the templates enclosed to prepare your abstract and paper. All submissions should be sent to yvsm@vef.gov by the deadline. Please notify the e-mail subjects as: YVSMabstract and YVSMpaper for the respective submissions and your suggestion for your paper presentation. Abstract acceptance by the Organizing Committee will be notified to the authors by e-mail.

Authors whose abstracts have been accepted by the Organizing Committee should prepare a full paper according to the format instructions for authors and send the paper in electronic format according to the Templates provided by the Publication Committee and the corresponding PDF files. These files will be used not only for conference reference, but also placed on the conference website for a wider audience.
